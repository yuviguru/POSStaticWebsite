SALVOS, one of the largest media solution providers in India, has been greatly acknowledged for its on-screen, off-screen, branding & promotional activities.

Established in 1989 by Shri. V. Arunachalam, SALVOS is engaged in the business of screening advertisements (Films & Slides) in theatres across the country and carrying out branding & promotional activities in Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala and Tamilnadu especially in rural markets.

SALVOS, with its own network of more than 20 branches across south india, has set high standards in service deliverables. Since establishment, SALVOS has been continuously upgrading its network to meet all requirements of its clients. SALVOS has become preferred media partners for majority of its clients.

As Part of SALVOS Team, you can experience personal & professional growth, challenging & exciting work plus outstanding rewards & recognition. SALVOS is ongoingly looking out for experienced and outstanding individuals who can relocate to any part of south India based on requirement.
